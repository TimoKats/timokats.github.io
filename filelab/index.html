Why FileLab?

Much of our public and private sector depends on data. As a result, applications that can work with data (like: Python, R and Excel) have become more and more important. However, these applications are also limited to structured data only.

Which is a problem, because the vast majority of data is actually unstructured, and therefore unattainable for these applications. That's why I created FileLab. Which can structure this data in a very easy and customizable manner.

I have already built a proof-of-concept that I demo in one of the videos below. Next, for further development, I'm looking for feedback and launching customers that are willing to try FileLab for free.
