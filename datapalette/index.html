About

DataPalette is a free no-code webscraping application for Windows. Its goal is to simplify webscraping and make it accessible for everyone. Because in a world where data is the new oil, everyone should be included.

If you support this goal feel free to download the application, give feedback, or donate to the developer. All help is much appreciated, thank you.

How it works

  • Meta data only

    DataPalette respects data ownership and only scrapes data that is explicitly meant to be public.

  • Collect URLs

    Automatically collect URLs based on links and custom lists. All possible with just one click!

  • Collect content from URLs

    Collect data from URLs and export it to CSV-format without writing a single line of code!
